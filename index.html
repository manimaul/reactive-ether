Reactive-Ether library

Broadcast and observe (share) resource objects

Reactive-Ether was created to easily share / persist non-serializable objects between Android Activities and Fragments where serializing resources in the Bundle is the standard mechanism during device configuration changes and Fragment/Activity communication.
